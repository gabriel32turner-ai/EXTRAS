My City

Describe the place where you live. The good, the bad, and everything in between.

🎪 Show & Tell 💉 Vocab 🔀 Choose & Speak 🃏 Questions ✨ My Sentence
Phase 1
🎪 Show & Tell
Show a photo of your city on your phone
5 min
Teacher Models First
"OK, look — this is my city. This is Celaya. It is in Guanajuato, Mexico. It is not very big but it is not small. The weather is nice — it is warm. I like it because it is my home. OK, now you — show me YOUR city or tell me about it."
Student Sentence Starters
My city is ___.
It is in ___ (state / country).
It is ___ (big / small / beautiful / industrial).
I like it because ___.
Capa 1 · Pedagogical
  • Most students are FROM their city but don't know how to describe it in English. This is a pride moment.
  • Common: "Celaya is a city of Guanajuato" → "Celaya is IN Guanajuato." Preposition fix.
  • If student is NOT from Celaya originally: "Where are you FROM? Is your original city different?" — opens up more language.
  • Accept "I like" as chunk. Also accept "The people are nice" — that is perfect A1.1 production.
Capa 2 · Engagement
  • "You just introduced your city in English! If a foreign visitor asks — you have the answer."
Capa 3 · Growth & Retention
  • "Now you can describe your phone, family, desk, food, house, AND city. That is a complete life description."

Phase 4
🃏 Question Cards
Teacher models first — then you answer
12 min
Question 1
What is the name of your city?
"My city is Celaya. It is in the state of Guanajuato. It is in the center of Mexico. It is an industrial city."
Now you: "My city is..."
Tip: If student is from Celaya, same answer works. If from another city, even better — new vocabulary emerges.
Question 2
Is your city big or small?
"Celaya is... medium! It is not very big like Mexico City but it is not small. There are maybe 500,000 people."
Now you: "My city is..."
Tip: Teach "medium" as a new word right here. Students love having the middle option — it is honest.
Question 3
How is the weather in your city?
"The weather in Celaya is nice. It is warm most of the year. Sometimes it is very hot in April. It is cold in December but not very cold."
Now you: "The weather is..."
Tip: Teach months as chunks: "in April", "in December". Point to the calendar. Simple and useful.
Question 4
Is there a good park in your city?
"Yes, there is Parque Xochipilli. It is nice and it is big. There are trees and a lake. It is a good place for families."
Now you: "There is..." / "There is no good park."
Tip: Use real Celaya/local references. If they mention a specific park, react: "Oh, is it beautiful?"
Question 5
Is the traffic in your city terrible or OK?
"The traffic is TERRIBLE! In the morning it is very bad. It is stressful. The boulevard is always full of cars."
Now you: "The traffic is..."
Tip: Traffic is an EMOTIONAL topic for every Mexican professional. Let them vent. They will produce a lot of language here.
Question 6
Is your city a good place to live?
"Yes, Celaya is a good place to live. It is not perfect — the traffic is bad and sometimes it is dangerous. But the food is amazing, the people are nice, and it is my home."
Now you: "My city is... to live because..."
Tip: This is the synthesis question. They combine everything: adjectives, "there is", opinions. Let them build a complete picture. No corrections — just celebrate the output.
Capa 1 · Pedagogical
  • This is the MOST complex Micro Lab so far — students must combine adjectives, "there is/are", locations, and opinions.
  • Watch for: "Is dangerous" (no subject) → "IT is dangerous." Subject pronoun enforcement.
  • "The people is nice" → "The people ARE nice." "People" is always plural — correct this one firmly.
Capa 2 · Engagement
  • "You just described your entire city in English — weather, traffic, parks, food. That is a full description!"
  • "A foreign colleague asks about Celaya — now you can answer. In English. That is power."
Capa 3 · Growth & Retention
  • "Six Micro Labs complete. You can describe your phone, family, desk, food, house, and city. That is YOUR world in English."
  • Testimonial trigger: "Think about Session 1 vs now. The difference is real."
Phase 5
✨ My Sentence
Build one perfect sentence — practice it — record it
5 min
Complete This Sentence
My city is ___ and it is a good place to live because the ___ is ___.
📱 WhatsApp Homework: Record this sentence as a voice note (30 seconds). Say it with pride — this is YOUR city. Send it to your teacher.
Capa 1 · Pedagogical
  • Model: "My city is Celaya and it is a good place to live because the food is amazing."
  • Alternative: "...because the people are nice." or "...because the weather is warm."
  • This sentence is LONGER than previous Micro Labs. They are ready — 6 sessions of building confidence.
Capa 2 · Engagement
  • "That is a complete sentence about your city. You own that sentence. Nobody taught it to you — you built it."
Capa 3 · Growth & Retention
  • "Six topics. Six perfect sentences. Six voice notes. You are not a beginner anymore — you are a speaker."
  • "Next level: you start DOING things in English — actions, routines, verbs. But today, be proud."
