My Favorite Place in My City

That coffee shop, that park, that corner you always visit. Describe it in English — what you do there and why you love it.

🎪 Show & Tell💉 Vocab🔀 Choose & Speak🃏 Questions✨ My Sentence
Phase 1
🎪 Show & Tell
Show a photo of your favorite place or describe it
5 min
Teacher Models First
"OK, my favorite place in my city is a coffee shop near my house. It is called La Cafetera. I go there every Saturday. I drink coffee and I read. It is quiet and the music is nice. I always sit near the window. OK — what is YOUR favorite place? Show me a photo or tell me about it."
Student Sentence Starters
My favorite place is ___.
I go there every ___ (weekend / Saturday / morning).
I usually ___ there (eat / drink / read / walk / sit).
I like it because ___.
Capa 1 · Pedagogical
  • Key difference from A1.1 My City: now they describe what they DO there, not just what the place IS. Verbs are active.
  • Push for frequency: "Do you go EVERY weekend or SOMETIMES?" Natural adverb practice.
  • If student says "I going there" → "I GO there." No present continuous at A1.2.
Capa 2 · Engagement
  • "A taco stand? That is a great favorite place! What do you eat there?"
  • "You sit near the window too? Same here! Window people understand each other."
Capa 3 · Growth & Retention
  • "You can now recommend a place to a foreign visitor — in English. That is a real skill."

Capa 1 · Pedagogical
  • MODEL pair 1: "I choose COFFEE SHOP. I go there on Saturdays. I drink a cappuccino and I read. I don't go to the park because I don't like the sun."
  • Push for WHY: "You go alone? Why?" — produces "because I like..." structures.
  • Watch for: "I go in the night" → "I go AT night." Time prepositions — quick fix.
Capa 2 · Engagement

Phase 4
🃏 Question Cards
Teacher models first — then you answer
12 min
Question 1
What is your favorite place in your city?
"My favorite place is a coffee shop called La Cafetera. It is small and quiet. I go there every Saturday."
Now you: "My favorite place is..."
Tip: Accept real local names. If it is a taco stand with no name: "the taco place on Main Street" is perfect.
Question 2
What do you do there?
"I drink coffee and I read my phone. Sometimes I eat a sandwich. I sit near the window and I relax."
Now you: "I usually... and I..."
Tip: Multiple verbs in sequence: "I drink, I read, I sit, I relax." Each one is a simple present win.
Question 3
Do you go alone or with someone?
"I usually go alone. I like my alone time. But sometimes I go with my wife on Sundays. We eat breakfast together."
Now you: "I usually go... because..."
Tip: "Alone time" — teach as a chunk. Mexican professionals value this but rarely have words for it in English.
Question 4
Is there a good restaurant near your house?
"Yes, there is a restaurant called El Rincón. The food is delicious and it is not expensive. I eat there on Fridays."
Now you: "There is a... near my house."
Tip: "There is" + description + what you do there. Three layers of language in one answer.
Question 5
Can you walk to your favorite place or do you drive?
"I can walk! It is only 10 minutes from my house. I don't drive because it is close. I like the walk — it is relaxing."
Now you: "I can walk / I drive because..."
Tip: "Can" in a real context — not a textbook exercise but an actual question about their life.
Question 6
Do you spend a lot of time there?
"Usually one or two hours. I don't spend all day. I go, I relax, and then I go home. It is my Saturday routine."
Now you: "I usually spend... there."
Tip: "Spend time" — key chunk. Also practice numbers: "one hour", "two hours", "thirty minutes".
Question 7
Do you recommend this place to other people?
"Yes! I always recommend it. The coffee is amazing and the atmosphere is perfect. Everybody likes it."
Now you: "I recommend... because..."
Tip: This is a PRESENTATION skill — recommending something. Useful for work and social life.
Question 8
What is special about this place for you?
"It is MY place. I go there and I forget about work. The world is quiet. I drink my coffee and I feel happy. It is simple but it is important for me."
Now you: "It is special because..."
Tip: Emotional peak. "I feel happy" — accept "feel" as a chunk verb. This is real, personal production. Celebrate it.
Capa 1 · Pedagogical
  • Grammar stars: simple present verbs (I go, I drink, I sit), "there is/are", "can/can't", frequency adverbs.
  • Watch for: "I'm going there every week" → "I GO there every week." Habitual = simple present.
Capa 2 · Engagement
  • "You just gave a complete tour of your favorite place — in English. A tourist would follow your recommendation!"
Capa 3 · Growth & Retention
  • "You can describe people at your office AND recommend your favorite place. That is real-world English."
  • Testimonial trigger: "Next time you go to that place, think: I described this in English. I can talk about MY world."
Phase 5
✨ My Sentence
Build one perfect sentence — practice it — record it
5 min
Complete This Sentence
I like ___ because I go there every ___ and I always ___.
📱 WhatsApp Homework: Record this sentence as a voice note (30 seconds). Say it like you are recommending it to a friend. Send it to your teacher.
Capa 1 · Pedagogical
  • Model: "I like La Cafetera because I go there every Saturday and I always drink coffee and relax."
  • Three clauses: place + frequency + action. Longest Micro Lab sentence so far.
  • Pronunciation: "be-CAUSE", "EV-ry", "AL-ways".
Capa 2 · Engagement
  • "That is a complete recommendation sentence. You can use it in real life — today."
Capa 3 · Growth & Retention
  • "Office people, favorite places — you can now describe your world with actions and details."
