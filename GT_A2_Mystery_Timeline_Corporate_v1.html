Correct Sequence with Logic

  1. Maria received an email from the client yesterday — The email is the START of the story. Everything begins here.
  2. She talked to her manager about the project — First internal action after receiving the email.
  3. They organized a meeting with the team — Decision made to involve the team — preparation begins.
  4. The team discussed the proposal — The meeting happens, discussion follows naturally.
  5. They made an important decision — After discussion, a decision is reached.
  6. Maria sent the final plan to the client — After the decision, the plan is ready to send.
  7. The client accepted the proposal — Last event: the outcome/result of the entire sequence.

Discussion Prompts — Use these if the group debates

"Why did Maria RECEIVE an email FIRST?" "What did she DO after she RECEIVED the email?" "When did they ORGANIZE the meeting — before or after the discussion?" "Why did the client ACCEPT at the end, not at the beginning?"

Follow-up Questions — After correct order

"Did Maria TALK to her manager BEFORE or AFTER the meeting?" "WHEN did the team DISCUSS the proposal?" "What HAPPENED after they MADE the decision?"
